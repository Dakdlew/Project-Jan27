Project: Raw Popup

ART BASEL MIAMI°

N°1

2018. December 4th - 9th Designed For: Raw Pop Up Burdines Flagship Downtown Miami, FL.

Interactive Installation exploring the nuclear family in the revitilazation of the mothership of department stores in 1950’s Miami. This technology based instalation taps into the cold war era psychological expirements through user interaction, allowing one to fulling submerse themselves in the exhibition and taking a piece of the past with them.
